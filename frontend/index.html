Frequently Asked Questions

How does QR code file sharing work?

Simply upload your file, and we'll generate a unique QR code. Anyone can scan this QR code with their phone camera to instantly download your file. It's perfect for sharing files between different devices quickly.

Is FileQRkaro free to use?

Yes! FileQRkaro is completely free to use. You can upload files up to 10MB and generate QR codes without any registration or payment required.

How long are files stored?

For security and privacy, all uploaded files are automatically deleted after 24 hours. This ensures your data doesn't remain on our servers longer than necessary.

What file types are supported?

FileQRkaro supports all file types including documents (PDF, DOC, TXT), images (JPG, PNG, GIF), videos (MP4, AVI), audio files, and compressed folders (ZIP, RAR).
